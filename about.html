About AJiusBlog

AJiusBlog started on a frustrated Tuesday. I'd just returned my third "ergonomic" keyboard, read a dozen identical listicles, and thought — someone should write the honest version. The one that admits when gear disappoints.

We're not a content farm. Every article is written by people who actually use the products, deploy the platforms, and sit through the bad Zoom calls. We publish fewer pieces, but each one is meant to save you time and money.

Whether you're setting up a home office, picking your next SaaS tool, or just keeping up with tech — welcome. Grab a coffee and stay a while.
